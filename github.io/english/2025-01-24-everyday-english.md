- Be good.
- Stay still.
- Let's have dinner.
- Let's have lunch.
- Let's have breakfast.
- Roll up your sleeves.
- Roll down your sleeves.
- Bring the pillow.
- Use an umbrella.
- Peel and eat.
- Take small bites and eat.
- Come up the stairs.
- Go down the stairs.
- I'm folding towels.
- I'm organizing towels.
- I'm folding clothes.
- Hold my hand => You can let go now.(손놓아도돼보다 자연스러운표현)
- Take it out. 그거 꺼내봐
- Take the balloon out (the balloon 을 강조) vs Take out the balloon (좀 더 일반적이고 take out 동사가 더 강조됨)
- Please, tie the ballon.
- poke it with a fork. (포크로 콕 찍다.)
- put down something. (무언가를 내려 놓다)
- pick up : 집어 올리다. (손이나 팔을 이용해 들어올리는 상황에서 많이 사용)
  - I need to pick up my keys. (열쇠를 집어들다.)
  - Can you pick me up at the airport? (공항에서 나 차에 태워줘)
- lift : 들어 올리다. (힘을 주어 물건을 높이올리는 상황에 사용)
- Dip it in ketchup and eat.
- put some ketchup on it and eat.
