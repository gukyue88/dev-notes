#### 과거 시간 표현 (어젯밤 vs 어제 아침/오후/저녁)

- 어젯밤에 뭐 먹었어? (last night)
  - What did you eat last night?
  - Where did you go last night?
  - Who did you meet last night?
- 어제 저녁(오후, 아침)에 뭐 먹었어? (yesterday + morning/afternoon/evening)
  - What did you eat yesterday morning?
  - What did you read yesterday evening?
  - Where did you go yesterday afternoon?
- 어제 저녁에 치킨 먹었어
  - I ate fried chicken last night.
  - I met Sally yesterday morning.
  - I mew peter yesterday afternoon.

#### 과거의 시점과 시간 표현

- 너 어디에서 언제 살았었어?
  - When did you live in Korea?
- 얼마 전에 어디에서 살았었어.
  - I lived in Korea three years ago.
  - I lived in the US five months ago.
- 언제부터 언제까지 어디에서 살았었어.
  - 연도를 말할 때, 2000~2009년은 두자리로 안끊고, 한 번에 읽음
  - I lived in Korea from 2010 to 2015.
  - I lived in China from 2002 to 2018 (2002: 투싸우전드투, 2019: 투웨니나인틴)
- 언제 어디에서 살았었어.
  - I lived in New York in 2014.
  - I lived in Korea in 1999.

#### 나 학교에 있었어!

- be동사의 과거형
  - I/he/she/It + was
  - you, they, those + were
- at으로 알리는 위치 : 일반적으로 어디에 있다고 알릴때(주변에 있던, 안에 있던 관계 없이)
  - She was at the hospital.
  - They were at the train station.
  - We were at the gym.
- in으로 알리는 위치 : 정확히 어디 안에 있다는걸 말할때, 사물이나 사람 모두 대상이 될 수 있음
  - I was in the post office.
  - They were in the classroom.
  - He was in the bathroom.
  - The wallet was in the pocket.

#### 너 방 안에 있었어?

- be 동사 과거형으로 질문하기
  - Were you at school?
  - Was he in the building?
  - Were they in the room?
  - Were you at work?
- 대답하는 법
  - Yes, I/He/She/It was
  - No, I/He/She/It wasn't
  - Yes, They/We were
  - No, They/We weren't
- 의문사로 질문하기
  - Where were you?
  - Where were they?
  - Who were they?
  - What was that?
  - When was your birthday party?
