#### 현재진행 (지금의 의미와 요즘의 의미와 모두 쓰임)

- right now : 지금
  - I'm watching Youtube right now.
- these days : 요새
  - I'm studying English these days.

#### 현재진행 질문/부정

- daydreaming. (생각에 곰곰히 잠긴)
- Are you ~ing?
  - Are you daydreaming?
  - Are you watching Youtube?
  - Are you having fun?
- I'm not ~.
  - I'm not daydreaming
  - I'm not watching Youtube.
  - I'm not having fun.
- Are you ~ing again?
  - Are you daydreaming again?
  - Are you watching Youtube?
  - Are you having fun?
- I'm really not ~ing.
  - I'm really not watching Youtube.
  - I'm really not daydreaming

#### 현재진행 의문사 질문

- Where
  - Where are you living?
  - Where are we staying?
- Who/What
  - What are you cooking?
  - Who are you helping?
- Why/How
  - Why aren't you drinking?
  - Why are we running?
  - How are you doing?
  - How are you feeling?

#### 과거 진행 (~하던중)

- What were you ~ing a moment ago? (방금 전에 뭐하고 있던 중이었어?)
  - What were you watching a moment ago?
  - What were you doing a moment ago?
- I was just ~ing. (just : 그냥 아무일도 아닌것을 강조할때)
  - I was just using speak.
  - I was just watching Youtube.
- I was just ~ing for ~.
  - I was just practicing for an interview.
  - I was just studying for a test.
