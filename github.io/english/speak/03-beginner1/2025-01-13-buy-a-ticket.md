#### 왕복 티켓 하나 주세요

- I'd like a ticket to ~ (I would like가 I want 보다 공손함. I want는 약간 요구하는 느낌)
  - I'd like a ticket to Lasvegas
  - I'd like two tickets to the museum.
- one-way (편도) / roundtrip (왕복)
  - I'd like a one-way ticket to Las vegas.
  - I'd like a roundtrip ticket to Miami.
- Do you offer any discounts?
  - Do you offer any discounts for students?
  - Do you offer any discounts for children?

#### 당일 패스는 얼마에요?

- How much is it for ~?
  - How much is it a shuttle?
  - How much is it for a single ride?
  - How much is it for a day pass?
- I'd like tickets for ~.
  - I'd like tickets for 2 adults and 1 child, please.
  - I'd like tickets for 2 seniors and two children, please.

#### 근처에 공원이 있나요?

- There's a pool.
- There's a gym.
- There's a great cafe nearby.
- There are some great restaurants nearby.
- Is there a grocery store nearby?
- Are there any parks nearby?
- There aren't any parks nearby. (any : not even one)

#### 모두를 위한 음식이 충분히 있나요?

- Audrey likes glamping.
- Audrey doesn't like camping.
- Is there enough food?
- Are there enough marshmallows?
- Is there enough food for three people?
- Are there enough snacks for four days?
- There's not enough.
- There's plenty. (plenty : more than enough)
