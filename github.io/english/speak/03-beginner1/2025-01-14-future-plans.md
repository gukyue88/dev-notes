#### 나는 파티를 열 거야!

- It was spontaneous. (즉흥적이었어)
- Do you have any plans for your birthday?
- Do you have any special plans for the holidays?
- I'm gonna throw a party. (파티를 열거야)
- I'm gonna go to my grandma's (할머니네 집에 갈꺼야. house는 가끔 생략됨)
- I'm gonna play it by ear. (상황봐가면서(즉흥적으로) 할꺼야)

#### 너 파티에 뭐 가져올 거야?

- Are you gonna come to the party?
- Are you gonna bring a present?
- Yes, I'm gonna be there.
- No, I'm not gonna make it.
- How are you gonna get there? (거기 어떻게 갈꺼야?)
- Who are you gonna bring? (누구 데려올꺼야?)

#### 내 휴가는 여유로울 거야.

- The final exam is gonna be really hard.
- My vacation is gonna be chill. (chill: 여유로운, 공식적인 자리에서는 쓰지 않음)
- I'm gonna be late for ~ (~에 늦을 것 같아)
  - I'm gonna be late for class.
  - I'm gonna be late for the meeting.
  - I'm gonna be late for work.
- Let's study together!
- Let's wrap up. (마무리 하자. 끝내자)
