#### 내가 피크닉에 담요를 가져올게 (will)

- I will.
- I'll bring a blanket.
- She'll bring wine and cheese.
- Will you bring a firsbee?
- Will you give me a ride?
- Will you give Audrey a call?

#### 너 여기 언제 도착해?

- Will you be late?
- Will you be here on time?
- When will you be ready?
- When will you be back?
- I'll be done in about 30 minutes.
- I'll be there in about 2 hours.

#### 내가 너에게 만회할게, 약속해! (will 약속)

- I blew it! (망쳤다)
- I totally blew it! (완전 망쳤다)
- I'll make it up to you. (내가 만화할께)
- I'll take you out to dinner.
- I'll make it up to you, I promise.
- I'll take you out to you, I promise.
- I won't break your heart.
- I won't forget our anniversary ever again.

#### 그녀는 분명 신나 있을거야! (bet : 내기하다의 의미도 있지만 여기에서는 '~거야'의 의미를 배움)

- I bet she's a good teacher.
- I bet he's really hungry.
- I bet she'll give him some treats.
- I bet she'll love it here.
- I bet he won't be hungry tonight.
- I bet she won't teach today.
