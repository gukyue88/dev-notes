#### 로컬 맛집 추천해주실래요?

- a good restaurant : 맛집 (delicious restaurant 는 틀린 표현임)
- a popular restaurant
- 맛집 추천해주실 수 있나요? (Can you 보단 Could you를 써서 공손한 표현)
  - Could you recommend a good restaurant?
  - Could you recommend some good places to visit?
  - Could you recomment a good cafe?
    - with a nice view? (전망이 좋은)
    - with a local vibes? (현지 느낌이 나는)
    - with a patio? (야외 테이블이 있는)
  - Could you recommend the best T-bone steak restaurant?
  - Could you recommend the best pasta restaurant?
  - Could you recommend the best udon restaurant?
- 어떤 걸로 유명해요? (be known for : ~로 알려진/유명한)
  - What are they known for?

#### 창가 테이블로 가능할까요?

- 예약하고 싶어요 (I want to 보단 I'd like to가 공손함)
  - I'd like to make a reservation
    - for 1pm
    - for Wednesday afternoon
    - for tomorrow at 6pm
  - How many guests and what time?
  - A party of 2 for 8pm (A party of 2 people에서 people을 생략함)
  - A party of 5 for noon
- 대기가 얼마나 긴가요?
  - I'd like to put my name on the waiting list.
  - How long is the wait?
- 야외 테이블에 앉을 수 있나요? (Is it possible to ~?)
  - Is it possible to
    - get a table outside?
    - get a table by the window?
    - get a booth? (booth는 푹신한벤치형태의자)
    - get a quiet table?

#### 메뉴 좀 주실래요?

- What do you recommend?
- ~이름으로 예약되어 있어요
  - I have a table booked for three under John Kim.
- 메뉴판 좀 주세요
  - Can we grab the menu, please? (grab: 건네받다)
  - Can we grab the drinks menu, please?
  - Can we grab the dessert menu, please?
- 시간이 더 필요해요
  - Are you ready to order?
  - We need a few more minutes.

#### 쌀국수, 고수 빼고 주세요.

- We're ready to order.
- ~로 주문 할게요
  - I'll get the calamari.
  - We'll get the Caesar salad and fish and chips.
  - starter/appetizer : 메인 요리 전 먹는 음식
    - We'll get the onion soup to start.
    - We'll get the bruschetta to start.
  - entree : 메인 요리
  - sides : 사이드 요리
    - For the entree, I'll get the meatball spaghetti.
    - For the sides, I'll get the mashed potatoes and asparagus.
  - without : ~ 빼고
    - I'll get the pho without the cilantro.
    - I'll get the cheeseburger without the onions.
- 미디엄 웰로 익혀주세요
  - How do you like your steak? (스테이크 어떻게 해드릴까요?)
    - rare, medium-rare, medium, medium-well, well-done
    - medium, please.
  - How do you kike your eggs?
    - scrambled : 휘저어서 익힌
    - over-hard : 완벽히 익힌
    - over-easy : 뒤집어서 노른자를 살짝 익힌
    - sunny-side up : 뒤집지 않고 흰자만 익힌, 노른자가 해처럼 동그란
    - poached : 깨뜨려서 삶은
      - please
- 알레르기가 있어요
  - Do you have any allergies?
    - Yes, I'm allergic to peanuts. (be allergic to ~)
    - Yes, I'm allergic to shellfish. (갑각류)

#### 앞접시 좀 갖다 주실래요?

- 직원과 눈이 마주쳤을 때, Excuse me. 라고 얘기해야하고, 테이블 담당에게 요청해야함
- 리필해주세요
  - Can I get a refill, please?
  - Can I get the check, please?
  - Can I get another beer, please?
- 포장해 갈 수 있나요?
  - Can I get a box to go? (음식을 싸갈 박스를 줄 수 있나요? 라고 보통 얘기함)
  - Can I get a drink to go? (미국에서는 리필되는 음료의 경우 싸가는 경우가 많음)
  - Can I get some utensils to go? (식기류를 몇 개 싸갈 수 있나요? 일회용을 붙이지 않아도 이해함)
- 앞접시 주세요.
  - Could you bring some extra plates, please?
  - Could you bring another fork, please?
  - Could you bring some more bread, please?
