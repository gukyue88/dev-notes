#### 입국 심사 통과하기 1탄

- May I see your passport?
- Here you go. (여기요)
- 방문 목적 말하기
  - So, What's the purpose of your visit?
  - I'm here to visit family.
  - I'm here to just travel.
  - I'm here to study.
  - I'm here on business. (사업차 왔어요)
  - I'm here on vacation.
  - I'm here on tour.
  - I'm here for a business conference.
  - I'm here for school.
- 직업 말하기 (신분이 확실한 사람인지 확인하려는 의도임)
  - What is your occupation? (직업이 무엇인가요?)
  - I'm a student.
  - I'm a stay-at-home mom. (요새는 house-wife 보단 stay-at-home mom 이라는 표현을 씀)
  - I run restaurant.
  - I run a startup.

#### 입국 심사 통과하기 2탄

- Where are you coming from?
- I'm coming from South Korea.
- 체류기간 : 이미 정해진 미래를 말할땐 현재진행을 씀
  - So, how long do you intend to stay?
  - I'm staying for 3 days.
  - I'm staying about a month.
  - I'm staying for just 5 days.
  - I'm leaving on June 13th.
  - I'm leaving on the 25th. (이번달 25일)
  - Do you have a return ticket?
  - Here's my itinerary. (itinerary : 여행 일정, 항공 일정표)
- 숙소
  - Where will you be staying?
  - I'm staying at the Hilton (Hotel 생략).
  - I'm staying at a friend's house.
  - I'm staying at an Airebnb.
  - Here's the address.

#### 환승편을 놓칠까 조마조마해요

- connecting flight / connection : 환승 항공편
- 곧 비행기 타야 해요
  - I have to catch a flight soon.
  - I have to catch a flight at 10 a.m.
  - I have to catch a flight in 30 minutes.
- 비행기 놓칠까봐 조마조마 해요
  - I'm afraid I might miss my flight.
  - I'm afraid I might miss my connecting flight.
- 얼마나 더 걸리나요?
  - How much longer will the food take?
  - How much longer will the wait be? (wait time을 줄여서 wait이라고 함)
  - How much longer will the flight be delayed?
- 앞으로 가도 될까요?
  - Can I go ahead?

#### 제 짐이 안 나왔어요.

- My suitcase hasn't arrived.
- 항공편 말하기
  - I was on flight KE21 from Seoul.
  - I was on flight WT23 from JFK. (JFK 공항)
- 가방 개수 말하기
  - How many bags are you missing?
  - Just one.
  - 5 total. (총 5개요)
- 가방 묘사하기
  - Can you describe the bags for me?
  - It's dark green.
  - It's medum sized.
  - It's a black Samsonite bag.
- 추가 질문하기
  - Do you have any idea where it is?
  - When will it arrive?

#### 복도 자리로 부탁해요

- 실례합니다, 어디에 있나요?
  - Excuse me, where is the check-in counter for Korean airline?
  - Excuse me, where is Terminal 3?
  - Excuse me, where is Gate 80?
- 한국으로 가요.
  - Where are you flying to today? (어디로 가시나요?)
  - I'm going to Korea. (이미 결정이 된 게획은 be ~ing으로 말하곤 함)
  - I'm flying to Toronto.
- 창가 쪽 자리를 원해요.
  - I'd like a window seat, please.
  - I'd like an aisle seat, please.
