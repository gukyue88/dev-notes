#### 에어비엔비, 게스트하우스

- Make yourself at home. (집처럼 편하게 지내세요)
- 일찍 체크인해도 될까요?
  - Can we check in earlier?
  - Can we check in around 11 a.m?
  - Can we check in later at night? (밤 늦게)
- 배달시켜도 괜찮아요?
  - Is it okay to get food delivered?
  - Is it okay to use the laundry? (laundry : 세탁물, 세탁실, 세탁소)
  - Is it okay to park in the garage?
  - Feel free!
- 어떻게 해야 하죠?
  - What should I do with the trash?
  - What should I do with the keys?
  - What should I do with the recycling?

#### 조식 시간이 어떻게 돼요?

- Hi, Check-in for Jenny. (제니로 체크인 할께요)
- 호텔 체크인하기
  - I need an ID and a credit card. (신분증과 신용카드)
  - Deposit (보증금은 나중에 알아서 취소 됨)
  - Here you go. (여기 있어요)
- 방 선택하기
  - I want a room with a king-size bed.
  - I want a room with double beds.
  - I want a room with an ocean view.
  - I want a room with a city view.
  - I want a room on a higher floor. (높은층)
  - I want a room on a lower floor.
- 서비스/시설 이용하기
  - amenities : Gym, Pool, Spa
  - What are your gym hours? (헬스장 영업 시간)
    - your hours : (너의 호텔의) 영업시간
    - your gym hours : 헬스장 운영시간
    - your pool hours : 수영장 운영시간
    - your breakfast hours : 조식 운영시간

#### 룸서비스 주문하고 싶어요.

- staycation (호캉스)
- 201호 인데요
  - Hi, I'm in Room 201. (3자 수는 한 자리씩 말하기 two oh one)
  - Hi, I'm in Room 1011. (4자리 수는 두 자리씩 끊어서 ten eleven)
  - Hi, Im in Room 1200. (00은 hundred. twelve hundred)
  - Hi, I'm in Room 400. (four hundred)
- 룸서비스 주문하고 싶어요
  - I'd like to order room service.
  - I'd like to order breakfast.
- 예약 잡아줄 수 있나요?
  - Could you arrange a ride for me? (ride : 이동수단)
  - Could you make a reservation for me? (for me : 나 대신의 의미)
  - Could you charge it to my room?

#### 에어컨이 안되는 것 같아요.

- Could we get a different room? (다른 방으로 바꿔줄 수 있나요?)
- 깨끗하지 않아요
  - The sheets aren't clean.
  - It smells like cigarettes.
  - It's too noisy.
- 작동 안 되는 것 같아요
  - It seems like the AC isn't working. (에어컨이 안되는 것 같아요)
  - It seems like the hot water isn't working.
  - It seems like the toilet is clogged. (변기가 막힌 것 같아요)
- 직원을 올려 보내줄 수 있나요?
  - Can you send someone up to fix this?
  - Can you send up some more towels?
  - Can you send up extra blankets?

#### 여기 짐 좀 맡겨 놔도 돼요?

- What time is check-out?
- 조금 늦게 체크아웃해도 돼요?
  - Can we check out a bit later?
  - Can we leave our bags here?
- 에어비엔비에서 체크아웃 하기
  - I'm checking out now!
  - Thank you for everything.
  - Thank you for providing breakfast.
  - Thank for giving me a ride.
