#### 저기요, 새치기하지마세요.

- Is this the line?
- 이게 줄이에요?
  - Is this the line for the exhibit? (전시회)
  - Is this the line for the ride? (놀이기구)
  - Is this the line for the show?
  - Is this the line for the tickets? (티켓팅)
- 티켓 얼마예요?
  - How much is the ticket?
  - How much is the ride?
  - How much is the tour?
  - How much is the discount?
- 티켓 2개 부탁해요
  - Three, please.
  - 2 adults, please.
  - 1 adult and 2 kids, please.
  - 4 students, please.
- 새치기하지 마세요
  - Excuse me, but I wase here first.

#### 여기 와이파이가 뭐예요?

- 실례가 안된다면...
  - Do you mind ~?
    - Yes, I mind. (네, 좀 꺼려져요)
    - No, I don't mind. (아니에요. 괜찮아요)
  - Do you mind taking a picture for us?
  - Do you mind helping us?
  - Do you mind if we take this chair?
  - Do you mind if I sit here?
- 혹시나 해서 여러 장 찍었어요
  - I took several just in case. (just in case : 혹시나 해서)
  - I took some sideways too just in case. (sideways: 가로로)
- 와이파이 비번이 뭐예요?
  - What's the Wi-Fi here?
  - What's the Wi-Fi password?

#### 분실물 센터가 어디 있나요?

- Where is the lost and found?
- 누가 훔쳐간 것 같아요
  - I think someone stole my iPad.
  - I think I lost my wallet at the mall.
  - I think I left my phone near the mens' room. (남자화장실)
  - I think I left my bag in the backseat.
- 검은색 지갑이에요
  - It's a black Chanel wallet.
  - They're bright red.
  - It's about this big. (손으로 크기를 말하면서, 이정도 크기에요)
- 그거 제 거예요
  - That's mine.
  - Could you leave your contact information?

#### 안전한 시티투어의 필수표현

- 제 물건 봐줄 수 있나요? (my stuff : 내 물건)
  - Can you watch my stuff?
  - Can you watch my stuff while I use the restroom?
  - Can you watch my stuff while I go get my coffee?
  - Can you watch my stuff while I take a call?
- 어떻게 갈 수 있나요?
  - Excuse me, how do I get to this place?
  - Excuse me, how do I get to Central Park?
  - Excuse me, how do I get to the nearest pharmacy?
- 근처에 있나요?
  - Is it far from here?
  - Is it nearby?
  - Is it within walking distance?
- 줄 게 없어요(일단 무반응이 우선 > 홈리스들이 다가왔을 때 대응하기)
  - Sorry, I don't have anything.
