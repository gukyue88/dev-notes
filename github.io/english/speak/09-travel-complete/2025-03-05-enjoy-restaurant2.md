#### 저기요, 파스타가 너무 불었어요

- 이거 저희가 주문한 거 아닌데
  - Excuse me, but this is not what we ordered.
  - Excuse me, but when will our food be ready?
  - Excuse me, but We never got our drinks. (영어로는 음식을 받지 않았다는 표현이 자연스러움)
- 면이 너무 불었어요
  - Excuse me, but this dish is too salty.
  - Excuse me, but this pasta is overcooked.
  - Excuse me, but this soup is too cold.

#### 계산서가 잘못됐어요

- Can I get the check, please?
- 따로 계산할게요
  - Is this together or separate? (계산 같이하시나요? 따로 하나요?)
  - We'd like to split the check.
  - It'll just be on one check. (그냥 계산서 하나에 넣어주세요)
- 계산이 잘 못됐어요
  - Excuse me, but what is this charge for? (charge : 계산 내역)
  - Excuse me, but you charged me twice. (중복 청구 됐어요)
  - Excuse me, but I never ordered this.

#### 1번 세트 메뉴로 주세요

- 1번 세트로 주세요
  - Can I get a Number 1?
  - Can I get a Number 3?
  - Can I get a Number 1 as a meal? (as a meal : 세트)
  - Can I get a Number 1, just the burger? (just the 메뉴 : 단품을 얘기할 땐 메뉴를 말함)
  - Can I get this warmed up? (데워주세요)
  - Can I get this in a seperate bag? (따로 포장해주세요)
- 어떤 옵션이 있나요?
  - What options do you have?
  - What breads do you have? (What breads == What kind of bread)
  - What toppings do you have?
- 음료 3개 주세요
  - Do you want to add any drinks to your order?
  - 2 fountain drinks, please. (fountain : 음료수 받는 기계)
  - No, I'm good. (괜찮아요! (친절한 거절))

#### 하우스 레드와인 한 잔 주세요

- a pint of draft beer (pint == 500cc)
- 어떤 음료가 있나요?
  - What juices do you have? (What kind of juice == What juices, 불가산인데 종류들을 의미하는거라 복수로 쓰임)
  - What soft drinks do you have? (soft drink : 탄산 음료)
  - What beers do you have?
  - draft beer, beer on tap(꼭지) : 생맥주
  - What do you have on tap? (생맥주는 뭐가 있나요?)
- 메뉴 받을 수 있을까요?
  - Can I get the drink menu?
  - drink, beverage : 술이 아닌 음료
  - Can I get the wine list?
  - I'll get a glass of the house red.
  - the house red, the house white
  - 맥주 단위
    - pint : 한 잔
    - pitcher : 피처
  - I'll get a pitcher of draft beer
  - I'll get a bottle of this wine
  - coke : 콜라 (코오-크 : 오를 길게 발길해야함)
  - sprite : 사이다
- 한 잔씩 더 부탁해요
  - We'll get another round, please. (같은걸로 하나씩 더 주세요. 술에만 해당함)

#### 톨 아이스 라테, 얼음 조금만 주세요

- 따뜻한 톨 캐러멜 라테 주세요. 시럽은 반만요
  - Hi, how's it going?
  - Good, thanks.
  - 사이즈 + 온도 + 메뉴 with 추가 내용
  - Can I get a small latte?
  - Can I get a 12 ounce vanilla latte?
  - Can I get a 12 ounce iced latte? (oz == ounce)
  - with light ice (얼음 조금만)
  - with extra ice (얼음 많이)
  - Can I get a tall iced latte with light ice?
  - Can I get a tall caramel latte with half the syrup?
  - with 2 pumps of vanilla : 바닐라 시럽 두 번
  - with almond milk
  - with whole milk (일반우유)
- 여기서 드시나요, 가지고 가시나요?
  - Is that for here or to go?
  - For here, please.
  - To go, please.
  - Your name? (맗하기 쉬운 이름으로 알려주면 좋음)
