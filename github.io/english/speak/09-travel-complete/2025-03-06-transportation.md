#### 거기 제 자리인 것 같아요 (기내영어)

- 죄송하지만, 저 화장실 좀 가야 해서요.
  - I'm sorry, but... (진짜 미안하다기보단 Excuse me의 역할)
  - I'm sorry, but I think that's my seat.
  - I'm sorry, but I think those are my headphones.
  - I'm sorry, but I just need to go to the restroom. (just: '좀'의 역할)
- 제 가방 좀 도와주시겠어요?
  - Could you help me with my bag, please?
  - Could you help me with this remote, please?
- 물 좀 갖다 주시겠어요?
  - Could I have some water, please?
  - Could I have an extra blanket, please?
- 죄송하지만, 좀 조용히 해주시겠어요?
  - I'm sorry, but could you pull down the blind, please?
  - I'm sorry, but could you keep it down a little, please? (be quiet는 명령조로 들림. keep it down a little을 대신 사용)

#### 비행기 시간을 바꾸고 싶어요

- I'm sorry. Could you repeat that?
- 제 항공편을 변경하고 싶어요
  - I'd like to change my flight date.
  - I'd like to change my flight time.
- 제 예약번호는...
  - What is your reservation number?
  - My reservation number is 3879.
  - My confirmation number is 2133.
  - V vs B
    - B as in boy. (boy할 때 b요)
    - V as in violin.
    - T as in tiger.
    - D as in dog.
  - My reservation number is T236. (T as in tiger, 2, 3, 6)
  - My reservation number is M342. (M as in mon, 3, 4, 2)
- 제 이름은 ...
  - My name is Kang. That's K.A.N.G.
  - My name is Min Joo. That's MIN as in Nancy, space J, O, O.
- 제일 빠른 비행기로 부탁해요
  - When would you like to chage your flight to?
  - The soonest flight from now.
  - The cheapest flight on the weekend.
  - The earliest flight on Friday.

#### 그냥 다음 신호등에서 내릴께요

- Where are you going?
- What's your price?
- ~까지 데려다줄 수 있나요?
  - Could you take me to Central Park?
  - Could you take me to the Hilton?
  - Could you take me to this address?
- 얼마 정도 나올까요?
  - Around how much would that cost?
- 여기서 내릴게요
  - I'll just get off here.
  - I'll just get off at this corner.
  - I'll just get off at the next light.
  - I'll just get off in font of the hotel.
- 얼마 드리면 되죠?
  - How much do I owe you?

#### 역까지 몇 정거장 더 남았나요?

- How much is the fare? (fare: 버스, 지하철, 택시등의 요금)
  - How much is the subway fare?
  - How much is the bus fare?
- 어디서 ~할 수 있나요?
  - Where can I catch bus number 10? (ride 보단 catch가 자연 스러움)
  - Where can I get a ticket?
  - Where can I get a transit card? (교통카드)
  - 버스 번호를 말할 때도 3자리가 넘어가면 끊어서 말하는게 자연스러움
    - 101 : one oh one
    - 806-2 : eight oh six dash two
- 어느 버스가 가나요?
  - Which bus goes to Times Square?
- 어디서 내려야 하나요?
  - Where shoud I get off?
  - Where shoud I get off to get to Central Park?
- 몇 정거장 남았나요?
  - How many more stops to Times Square? (stops: 버스나 지하철 정거장)
  - Do you konw how many more stops to Soho? (좀 더 공손하게 물어보기)

#### 런던행 기차는 어느 승강장에서 출발하나요?

- 왕복티켓 주세요
  - a one-way ticket (편도 티켓), a round-trip ticket(왕복 티켓)
  - Can I get a round-trip ticket to Paris, please?
  - Can I get 3 one-way tickets to Seattle, please?
- ~에 가고 싶습니다
  - I'd like to go Tuesday at 2 p.m.
  - When would you like to return?
  - I'd like to return Friday evening.
- 어느 승강장에서 출발하나요?
  - Which platform does the train to Lodon depart from?
  - Which platform does the train to Venice depart from?
