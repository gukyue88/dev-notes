#### 서비스로 열쇠고리 하나 주세요

- I'll take it! (이거 살께요)
- 얼마예요?
  - How much is this?
  - How much is this shirt?
  - How much is the total?
  - How much are these jeans?
- 2개에 10불 어때요?
  - How about 2 for 10? (2 bags for 10 dollars를 생략한 표현)
  - How about 3 for 20?
- 서비스로 주면 살게요
  - I'll take it if you throw in this hat.
  - I'll take it if you throw in this key chain.

#### 전 피부가 잘 뒤집어져요

- I'm just looking around. (그냥 둘러보는 거예요)
- 피부가 ~하는 편이에요
  - My skin tends to break out easily. (쉽게 뒤집어지는 편)
  - My skin tends to dry out easily. (쉽게 건조해지는 편)
  - My skin tends to get really oily. (기름이 많이생기는 편)
- ~을 찾고 있어요
  - Are you looking for anything specific?
  - I'm looking for a cushion foundation.
    - 쿠션 : cushion foundation
    - 가루제형 : powdery texture
    - 크립제형 : creamy texture
    - 섀도우 : eyeshadow
  - I'm looking for some eyeshadow with a creamy texture.
  - I'm looking for a red lipstick that is glossy. (glossy : 촉촉한, matte : 매트한)
- 다른 것도 있나요?
  - Do you have this in a different shade? (다른 색조도 있나요?)
    - lighter shade (밝은 색조)
    - darker shade (어두운 색조)
  - Do you have this lipstck in a lighter shade?
  - Do you have something closer to my skin tone?
  - Do you have any samples?
- 발라봐도돼요?
  - Can I try this on? (입어봐도/발라봐도/신어봐도/차봐도 될까요?)

#### 이 재킷 세일 중인가요?

- Are there any specials going on? (진행되는 특별 이벤트가 있나요?)
- 전부 50% 할인인가요?
  - Is everthing 20% off?
  - Is everthing 50% off?
  - an additional % off of the sale price (세일 가격에서 추가 % 깍은 가격)
  - Is it an additional 30% off of the sale price?
  - Do I get a free pouch if I spend over $50?
  - DO I get free samples if I spend over 100 dollars?
- 이거 세일 중인가요?
  - Is this sweater on sale?
  - Are these shoes on sale?
  - for sale vs on sale (for sale : 판매중, on sale : 세일중)
  - Are these jackets 2 for 20?

#### 뽁뽁이로 싸줄 수 있나요?

- Did anyone help you today?
- Yes, Eric helped me.
- 쇼핑백을 더 받을 수 있나요?
  - Can I get some extra bags?
  - Can I get some extra tissue paper(포장할때 쓰는 얇은 습자지)
  - Can I get a receipt?
  - Can I get a tax refund?
- 뽁뽁이로 싸줄 수 있나요?
  - Could you bubble wrap it? (뽁뽁이포장)
  - Could you gift wrap it? (선물포장)
- 카드 가입 거절하기
  - Did you want to make a card with us and save 20% off your order today?
  - No, thank you.
  - Are you sur?
  - Yes, I'm sure.

#### 이 셔츠 반품하고 싶어요

- What is your return policy?
- 환불받을 수 있나요?
  - Can I get a refund?
  - Can I get store credit? (상점 기프트카드나 포인트로 주는 경우도 있음)
- 반품하고 싶어요
  - I'd like to return this.
  - I'd like to return this shirt. (반품)
  - I'd like to exchange these pants. (교환)
- 얼룩이 있어요
  - What's the reason for the return?
  - There's a stain. (stain : 얼룩)
  - There's a tear. (tear: 찢어진 부분)
  - It doesn't fit right. (핏이 맞지 않아요)
  - I just changed my mind. (변심)
