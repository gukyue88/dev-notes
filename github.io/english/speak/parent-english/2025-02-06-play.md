- Mommy, I want to play~
- What do you want to ~
  - What do you want to play?
  - What do you want to make?
  - What do you want to draw?
  - What do you want to eat?
- Let's play ~
  - Let's play hide and seek!
  - Let's play tag! (tag : 술래잡기)
  - Let's play hospital (병원 놀이 하자)
  - Let's play house (집 놀이하다)
- Let's play with ~
  - Let's play with legos.
  - Let's play with dolls.
  - Let's play with cars.
- That sounds ~
  - That sounds fun.
  - That sounds exciting!
  - That sounds great! (잘된것 같다. 좋겠다.)
- Do you want to ~
  - Do you want to draw a car?
  - Do you want to build a snowman? (build : 눈사람처럼 여러 파트를 조합해 만들 때 사용)
  - Do you want to draw this?
  - Do you want to draw a princess?
  - Do you want to draw a snowman?
- origami : 종이접기
- Let me ~
  - Let me show you. (내가 보여줄께)
  - Let me help you. (내가 도와줄께)
- 칭찬
  - Wow look at that! (대단한걸 봤을 때, 감탄사)
  - That's an awesome car.
  - That's an awesome airplane.
- Who's going to be ~ (누가 ~ 할래?)
  - Who's going to be it? (it: 술래)
  - Who's going to be a doctor?
  - Who's going to be a nurse?
- I'll count to 20. (20까지 셀꺼야)
- Ready or not, here I come. (숨박꼭질에서 찾기 전에 술래가 꼭 하는 말)
- 애가 어디 있을까?
  - Where could he be?
  - Where could they be?
  - Where could she be?
  - Where is Minsu?
- Are you done with ~?
  - Are you done with the food? (밥 다 먹었어?)
  - Are you done with work? (일 다 끝났어?)
  - Are you done with the toys? (장난감 다 가지고 놀았어?)
  - Are you done with your books? (책 다 봤어?)
  - Are you done with your legos? (레고 다 가지고 놀았어?)
- It's ~ time.
  - It's clean up time.
  - It's snack time.
  - It's bath time.
  - It's lunch time.
- Let's ~ so we can ~.
  - Let's clean up so we can go to the park. (공원갈 수 있게 치우자)
  - Let's clean up so we can eat icecream.
  - Let's clean up so we can eat snacks.
- Let's play on ~. (~ 타고 놀자)
  - Let's play on the swings. (보통 그네는 여러개라 swings가 붙음)
  - Let's play on the slide. (slide : 미끄럼틀)
  - Let's play on the seesaw.
- It's not okay to ~ (~ 하는건 좋지 않아) : ~하지마가 아니라 ~하는건 좋지않아라고 말함.
  - It's not okay to throw things at others. (다른 사람에게 물건 던지는건)
  - It's not okay to push others. (다른 사람을 밀치는건)
- Are you ready to ~
  - Are you ready to go home?
  - Are you ready to go?
  - Are you ready to go to school?
